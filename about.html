Hobbies & Interests

Motorcycling

While my motorcycle primarily serves as my daily mode of transport across Singapore, it's also the centerpiece of my social life. I frequently join friends for group rides through the city, which fosters camaraderie and shared adventure. I am fully aware of the inherent dangers of riding, but I accept this risk because the freedom and clarity it offers are invaluable. When stress accumulates, I find profound relief in solo night rides, where the rhythm of the engine and the open road allow me to decompress and find creative inspiration.

Muay Thai & Boxing

Gym: Evolve MMA (Kinex)

Muay Thai is a cornerstone of my routine, offering a potent blend of physical fitness and mental fortitude. The rigorous training instills deep discipline and unwavering focus, qualities that extend beyond the gym. I find the sport immensely enjoyable and fun, and it keeps me in peak condition. The culmination of this training was participating in a fight night, an experience I thoroughly embraced and look forward to repeating.
